The Best Movies According to Lauren

My Top 3 Movies of All-time

2001: A Space Odyssey

Best visual effects and soundtrack. Makes you think about humanity and it's origins.

All About Eve

Great story and acting. Well put together and one of the greatest catchphrases of all time.

Terminator 2: Judgement Day

Great action/sci-fi movie. Great visual effects and storyline. Also one of the best sequels of all-time.
